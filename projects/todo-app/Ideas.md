# Ideas — To-do App

> Living list of ideas being considered, discussed, decided, or implemented.
> Add an entry whenever a half-baked idea surfaces. Mark `implemented` when
> shipped, `rejected` when killed.
> Approved ideas with concrete next steps move to [[Decisions]] when locked in.

## Status legend
- `open` — raw idea, not yet discussed
- `discussing` — actively being debated
- `approved` — decision made, awaits implementation
- `implemented` — shipped
- `rejected` — decided against
